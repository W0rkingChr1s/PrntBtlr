How the scan button works

Press the scan button on the printer and PrntBtlr runs scan2pdf.sh: it scans (ADF if loaded, otherwise the glass), builds a PDF, and drops it into the shared folder below.

On a Canon PIXMA, press SCAN → PC and then Color for a colour PDF or Black for a grayscale one. If the printer shows “Processing…” but no PDF appears, see the scan-button troubleshooting in the README.
